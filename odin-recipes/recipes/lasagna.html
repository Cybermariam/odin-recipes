Lasagna

Description

A delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. Heat the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread a layer of mozzarella cheese on top of the noodles.
  8. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  9. Bake for 30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
