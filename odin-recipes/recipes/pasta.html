Pasta

Description

A simple and delicious Italian dish made with pasta and your choice of sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the tomato sauce in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in the cooked pasta and toss to coat with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
