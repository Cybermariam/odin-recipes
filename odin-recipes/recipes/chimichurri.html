Chimichurri

Description

A vibrant Argentine sauce made with herbs, garlic, and vinegar, perfect for drizzling over grilled meats.

Ingredients

  • Fresh parsley
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Finely chop the parsley and cilantro.
  2. Mince the garlic and add it to a bowl.
  3. Add the chopped herbs to the bowl with the garlic.
  4. Whisk in the vinegar and olive oil until well combined.
  5. Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator for later use.
